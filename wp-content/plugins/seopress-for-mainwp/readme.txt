== Installation ==

1. Upload 'wp-seopress-mainwp' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to your MainWP dashboard, Extensions, SEOPress and start applying settings to your site.
